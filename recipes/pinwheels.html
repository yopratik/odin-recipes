Rainbow Veggie Pinwheels

Description

Rainbow Veggie Pinwheels are vibrant, bite-sized wraps filled with colorful vegetables and a creamy spread. They’re visually appealing, easy to make, and perfect for parties, lunchboxes, or as a healthy snack. The soft tortilla holds together crisp bell peppers, carrots, cucumber, and spinach, giving a crunchy, refreshing bite every time.

These pinwheels are versatile — you can swap veggies, add cheese, or even include a protein like turkey or hummus. They’re fun to make and even more fun to eat, offering a burst of color and flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (optional)
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • ½ red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • ½ yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix softened cream cheese with mayonnaise (if using), salt, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Layer the vegetables (carrot, cucumber, bell peppers, spinach) evenly on top of the spread.
  4. Roll each tortilla tightly into a log and wrap in plastic wrap. Chill for 15–20 minutes.
  5. lice each rolled tortilla into 1–1.5 inch pinwheels and serve immediately.
