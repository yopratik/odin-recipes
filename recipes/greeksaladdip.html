Greek Salad Dip

Description

Greek Salad Dip is a fresh, zesty appetizer inspired by the classic flavors of the Mediterranean. It combines crisp cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, tangy feta cheese, and briny olives into a vibrant, colorful dip that’s both healthy and satisfying. The combination of textures, crunchy vegetables with creamy feta, makes every bite refreshing and flavorful. Perfect for parties, picnics, or as a quick snack, it’s a dish that’s as visually appealing as it is delicious.

This dip is easy to prepare and versatile in how you serve it. Drizzled with olive oil and lemon juice and seasoned with oregano, it carries a bright, tangy taste that pairs wonderfully with pita chips, crackers, or fresh vegetables. Greek Salad Dip captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine, offering a simple yet elegant way to enjoy fresh ingredients with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and slice the Kalamata olives; place all in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently until evenly coated.
  4. Fold in the crumbled feta cheese carefully, mixing lightly.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for 15–20 minutes before serving with pita chips or crackers.
